Monarch Mission Statement

Our mission at Monarch Collective Studios is to help every client to transform into their best selves both inside and out.

We believe in continuous education, growth, and kindness. Our goal is that every client leaves happier than when they came in.

Monarch's No-Cash Refund Policy:

Monarch Collective will grant no monetary refunds of services for any reason. However, aternative refund options, such as store credit or exchange of services of an equal or lesser value, may be offered instead.
